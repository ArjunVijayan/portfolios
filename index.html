Design Philosophy

Construction
before ornament.

Every piece begins on the cutting board, not the sketchbook — form follows the pattern.

"I focus on precise pattern-making, reverse-engineering complex forms, and bridging the gap between rigorous construction and refined aesthetics."

— Gautham Prasad
Discipline
Leather product design — pattern engineering for wallets, card holders, and structured small goods.
Approach
Reverse-engineering existing forms to understand load, fold, and stitch geometry before drafting an original pattern.
Focus
Compact carry systems — card capacity, cash organization, and closures — built to hold shape over years of use.
Featured Project

Bifold Wallet — Men's Collection

Full-grain leather, hand-stitched in oxblood thread against a black shell.

A compact bifold built around a six-slot card system and a bucket-style cash compartment, closed with a magnetic latch. The oxblood interior is deliberate — a flash of color that only reveals itself when the piece is in use.
